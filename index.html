Good to Know

A few things to keep in mind for a smooth trip

☀️
Sunscreen is non-negotiable
The Caribbean sun is intense, especially on boat day. Bring SPF 50+ and reapply generously, bring a reef-safe formula too.
💧
Stay hydrated
Drink water constantly! Between the heat, dancing and cocktails, you'll need it. Carry a reusable bottle everywhere.
📱
Set your alarms
Days 2, 3 and 4 all have morning start times. Set two alarms the night before and agree on a group WhatsApp check-in.
💃
Bring pesos & card
Many local spots are cash only. Exchange Colombian pesos with your bank before leaving as it's typically cheaper than exchanging abroad. Keep small bills handy for tips and street food.
